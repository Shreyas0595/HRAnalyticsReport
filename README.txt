Overview
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a detailed view of employee attrition trends across demographics, education, salary bands, and tenure. Built in Power BI, it helps HR teams and leadership identify workforce challenges, monitor attrition rates, and make data-driven decisions to improve retention.

Purpose:-
- This dashboard enables HR teams to:
- Identify high-risk groups for attrition.
- Analyze attrition patterns by age, education, and salary.
- Pinpoint departments or roles with higher turnover.
- Support strategic workforce planning and retention initiatives.

Usage:-
- Open the Power BI dashboard.
- Apply filters for Department (HR, R&D, Sales) to focus analysis.
- Review attrition breakdowns by demographics, salary, and tenure.
- Use insights to design targeted retention strategies.

Benefits:-
- Clear visibility into workforce attrition drivers.
- Data-backed HR decision-making.
- Improved employee retention strategies.
- Enhanced organizational stability and growth.
